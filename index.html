🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard

Objective: To analyze pizza sales performance, identify top-selling products, and understand customer purchasing trends to optimize menu offerings and marketing strategies.

Observation: Classic pizzas lead slightly in revenue share (26.9%), but Veggie pizzas are close behind (25.46%), suggesting balanced customer preferences. “The Classic Del” is the clear bestseller, while niche pizzas like “Brie Carre” sell fewer units. Insights suggest focusing marketing on hero products, reconsidering low-demand sizes, and improving color consistency across charts.

Revenue by Size (Bar Chart): Large (L) pizzas generate the highest revenue, followed by Medium (M). XXL and XL contribute very little, which could indicate low demand or pricing issues. Observation: This suggests customers prefer standard sizes over extremes — useful for inventory and pricing strategy.

Orders by Category (Bar Chart): Classic pizzas have the highest total orders, aligning with their revenue share. Chicken and Supreme categories are close, while Veggie lags slightly in orders despite strong revenue — possibly higher price per unit. Observation: Veggie pizzas may have fewer orders but higher margins.

🩺 STI Health Data Analysis Dashboard

Project Overview: This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze and visualize sexually transmitted infection (STI) data across multiple institutions. The dashboard presents key health indicators such as STI status distribution, treatment types received, condom usage, and STI symptoms by infection type. It supports data-driven public health decision-making by revealing trends, risks, and treatment effectiveness.

Status by STI Type (Bar Chart): HIV shows the highest positive (+VE) cases compared to other STIs. Chlamydia and Gonorrhea also have notable positive counts, while HPV and Syphilis appear lower. Observation: HIV remains the most critical focus area, but bacterial STIs (Chlamydia, Gonorrhea) are also significant.

Treatment Received (Bar Chart by Gender): Antibiotics dominate treatment, especially for males. Females show higher use of topical creams and antifungals compared to males. Observation: Treatment distribution reflects the nature of infections (bacterial vs fungal/viral) and possibly gender-specific health-seeking behavior.

General Observation: Condom usage is nearly balanced (51.6% Yes vs 48.4% No), highlighting risk exposure. Symptom overlap (discharge, painful urination, fatigue) complicates diagnosis, reinforcing the importance of testing. The dashboard emphasizes urgent needs for awareness, prevention, and accessible treatment programs.

🍔 Fast Food Sales Dashboard

Objective: To monitor fast food chain performance, compare branch-level sales, and evaluate the impact of pricing and promotions on customer engagement.

Observation: Fast food dominates sales (68.6%) compared to beverages (31.4%). Afternoon and Evening are the strongest sales periods for both fast food and beverages, while Midnight and Night sales are minimal, suggesting limited late-night demand. Staffing and inventory can be optimized around Afternoon/Evening peaks. Sandwiches and Frankies lead fast food sales, while Cold Coffee and Sugarcane Juice top beverages. Online transactions are stronger for beverages, while cash dominates fast food purchases. Insights suggest focusing promotions on hero items, optimizing staffing during peak hours, and leveraging online beverage sales.
